How to make Sushi

  1. Collect sushi rice, nori (seaweed sheets), fresh fish or vegetables, soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
  2. Cook the rice and let it cool. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then fold this mixture into the cooled rice
  3. Slice fresh fish or vegetables into thin strips
  4. Add your chosen fillings along the center of the rice. Roll the sushi tightly
  5. Once rolled, use a sharp knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Enjoy your homemade sushi!
