How to make Steak

  1. Pat the steak dry with paper towels and season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. For a pan, add a small amount of oil with a high smoke point
  3. Sear each side for a few minutes until a golden-brown crust forms.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and continue cooking the steak to your preferred doneness.
  5. Once cooked, transfer the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Enjoy your perfectly cooked steak!
