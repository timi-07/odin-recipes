How to make Lasagna

  1. Gather lasagna noodles, ground beef and Italian herbs.
  2. Follow the package instructions to cook the lasagna noodles. Drain and set aside.
  3. Brown the ground beef or sausage with onions and garlic in a skillet. Add tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, beaten eggs, fresh basil, and Parmesan cheese
  5. In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, cooked noodles, and ricotta mixture
  6. Bake until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
